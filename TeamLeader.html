Team Leader/ Supervisor:

A Team Leader, often known as a supervisor or a Team Manager, is responsible for managing a team of agents. The Team Leaderâ€™s responsibilities are to ensure individual agents are performing against targets, while reviewing their performance and coaching them to do so. There is much more to the position than just that, though, as multi-tasking is very much central to the role. For example, Team Leaders are also expected to meet on a regular basis with their Manager, both to provide feedback and to receive instructions or actions A general list of duties, including keeping agents informed of what is happening in the center and anything that may affect them, is listed below.

Main Responsibilities

  1. Setting targets for both individuals and teams.
  2. Measuring the performance of both individuals and teams.
  3. Offering agents guidance and feedback.
  4. Helping to optimise procedures.
  5. Finding ways to motivate agents.
  6. Ensuring agents adhere to company policy, e.g. attendance.
  7. Updating, and preparing performance reports for, the Call Center Manager.

Important Skills:

  • Customer focused.
  • Expert product/service and job knowledge.
  • Dependability.
  • Clear communicator.
  • Multi-tasking.
  • Organised.
