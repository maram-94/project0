Contact Center Manager

This is the person who is ultimately responsible for the overall performance and budget of the center. They will make decisions regarding the people, the processes, the technology and the customers that are part of the call center and the future strategy and development of the center. In addition to this, the Call Center Manager is usually the person who liaises with other departments such as HR, IT.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Being responsible for performance management (e.g. setting objectives and reviewing KPIs).
  • Identifying areas of under performance.
  • Creating performance improvement plans.
  • Conducting internal reviews.
  • Ensuring clear communication across the call center.
  • Putting together business cases for extra funding and technology.
  • Creating and maintaining a positive culture.

Important Skills:

  • Influential leader.
  • Adaptable to changing business priorities.
  • Self-motivated.
  • Good communicator.
